Prep Your Skin

Neutrogena’s acne cleansing face wash adds the necessary hydration to your skin. Just like the glowing reflection of light against a shiny piece of glass, this oil settles in your skin and gives out the same kind of glow. It is non-sticky, so that your skin doesn’t feel unnecessarily heavy or greasy. Filled with ingredients like wheatgerm and camelina, it adds a moisturizing effect to your skin protecting it from dryness and signs of ageing. It is a multipurpose pre-makeup oil that can even be used as a regular oil moisturizer or replace the use of a primer. This revolutionary oil will bust all your oil-related myths and become your skin’s best friend in absolutely no time!

After cleansing and toning in the morning, gently apply to face and neck with clean hands. Best used in conjunction with the Brightening Night Comfort Cream for your nighttime routine.
